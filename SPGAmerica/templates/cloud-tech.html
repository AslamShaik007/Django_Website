Overview

For forward-thinking enterprises, cloud computing is the greatest solution because it offers pay-as-you-go on-demand computing resources. A wide range of global computing, storage, database, analytics, application, and deployment services are provided by Amazon Web Services (AWS), a secure industry leader in the cloud. AWS helps organisations automate and centralise their data and CRM functionality by providing services to over a million active clients each month across 200 countries.
