Single Sign-On

Now, you can automatically log into your Vitel Global account with your company credentials.

Take Off

Security at Your Control

In order to give your users a secure means to access the company account, Vitel’s single registration feature makes use of the security of your own network.

Additional Security Measure

To address some of the security parameters, VoIP SSO systems often implement additional security measures, such as multi-factor authentication (MFA), to ensure that users are who they claim to be.

New Accounts in Vitel Global

When a new log in happens, an activation email is provided to new users that includes their temporary password and PIN. Also, they will receive instructions for connecting into their new accounts using Vitel Global Single registration.

Benefits Users and Businesses

VoIP SSO can provide benefits for both users and businesses. But it is important to implement SSO in a secure and responsible manner. Deploy this with appropriate security controls in place to mitigate any potential risks.

Technology adapts to Simplicity

Use a single set of verified credentials to log in and access a variety of work applications and tools.

Better Employee Experience

Automatic logins with authentication eliminate user friction, improve the user experience overall, and encourage the adoption of work apps.

Benefits of Single Registration

  • Technical Efficiency

    With Vitel’s SSO, your employees no longer have to manage several usernames and passwords. This feature can reduce the workload on your IT employees when passwords need to be recovered.

  • Convenience

    So, your employees can log in to a VoIP system using a single set of credentials and this can be more convenient than having to remember multiple passwords.

  • Security

    By using a single set of credentials, the risk of password reuse or weak passwords is reduced. This facility allows the implementation of stronger password policies and multi-factor authentication.

  • Simplified user management

    Vitel Global’s SSO can make it easier to manage user access to multiple systems and applications and it can be particularly useful for businesses with large numbers of employees.

  • Improved productivity

    With SSO, users can quickly and easily access the VoIP system in order to reduce the time and effort required to log in to multiple systems.
