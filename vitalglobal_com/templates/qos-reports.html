Benefits of VoIP Quality of Service (QoS)

  • Mean opinion score (MOS)

    This is a subjective measure of the perceived quality of the voice or video by the end-user.

  • Latency

    This measures the delay between when a speaker speaks and when the listener hears the message. High latency can cause communication issues, such as delays or interruptions.

  • Jitter

    This measures the variation in the delay of voice or video packets transmitted over the network. High jitter can result in choppy or garbled audio or video.

  • Packet loss

    This measures the number of data packets that are lost during transmission. Even a small amount of packet loss can cause degradation in voice or video quality.
