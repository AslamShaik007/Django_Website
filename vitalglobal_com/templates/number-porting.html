Benefits of Number Porting

  • Retain Existing Phone Number

    Keep your existing phone number when switching to a new service provider. This is particularly useful for businesses that have established their brand around a specific phone number.

  • Reduce Costs

    Cloud services are often more cost-effective than traditional landlines. And, number Porting can allow users to switch to a new provider and take advantage of the cost savings.

  • Greater Flexibility

    VoIP services often come with a range of advanced features and capabilities. Number Porting allows users to take advantage of these features while still keeping their existing phone number.

  • Improved Business Continuity

    In the event of a natural disaster or other emergency, businesses may need to quickly switch to a new service provider. Number Porting can make this process easier and faster. It allows businesses to continue operating without interruption.

  • Mobility

    Cloud services can be used from anywhere with an internet connection. Number Porting allows users to take their phone number with them wherever they go, making it easier to stay connected while traveling or working remotely.
