Vermont, located in the northeastern region of the United States, is a state known for its picturesque landscapes, progressive values, and strong sense of community. Vermont's geography is defined by its iconic Green Mountains, part of the larger Appalachian Mountain range, that extend from the southern to the northern regions of the state. The state is also home to several lakes, including Lake Champlain, which forms a natural border between Vermont and New York. The state's natural beauty changes with the seasons, attracting visitors during the vibrant fall foliage, snowy winters ideal for skiing, and pleasant summers perfect for hiking and other outdoor activities.
