Answer From Any Device

With Simultaneous Ring feature, you can answer from any device it sends incoming calls to all of your connected devices at the same time. You can choose to answer calls on your desk phone, laptop or smartphone depending on where you are and what device you're working on.
 

How Simultaneous Ring Works

We enable Simultaneous Ring on all the devices so that your smartphone, desktop, laptop, desk phone can all ring at the same time when you're getting a call. Once you answer an incoming call or send it to voicemail, the ringing stops across all your devices.

Simultaneous Ring is an invaluable feature for anyone who's constantly on the go and needs to stay connected. However, it looks very similar to call forwarding but is not the same, where your business calls are forwarded to an external number.
