The following check list should be kept in mind while using Softphones and Mobile Apps:

  • Good Internet Connectivity 4G/ Wi-Fi
  • Compatibility of handsets as few non branded android handsets might not support our application as expected.
  •  Background applications or downloads may affect the voice quality
  •  Software/ OS updates during the calls may affect the voice quality
  • Port block by ISPs or service provider will lead to connectivity issues
  • Non-Compatible router can also lead to connectivity issues
  • Firewalls on the PCs will sometimes restrict an application from working, you need to disable the firewall.
  • Connecting issues, Call drop, voice breaks, voice echo is majorly due to Internet Issues.

As we all know softphones/ mobile apps are just for the mobility purpose and we always recommend you to use IP phones which are directly connected to internet for better voice quality.
